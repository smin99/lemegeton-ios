# Lemegeton

### A Grimoire Companion for Blood on the Clocktower

**Lemegeton** is a specialized note-taking and game-tracking application for the social deduction game *Blood on the Clocktower*. This app serves as your digital grimoire, helping you track claims, deduce roles, and win the game.

-----

## 👁️ Features

  - **Player Circle Tracking**: Map out the seating arrangement of your local game with a dynamic positioning UI.
  - **Role Claim Management**: Quickly assign and update role claims for every player.

## 🔒 Privacy Policy

Lemegeton is built with a "Privacy by Design" philosophy.

 * Zero Data Collection: The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or analytics. Your notes and game sessions remain yours alone.

 * Offline Functionality: The app works entirely offline. No internet connection is required to manage your grimoire or load scripts.

 * Local Storage Only: All game data and custom configurations are stored locally on your device and are cleared when you choose to reset a session or delete the app.
